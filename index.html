Sheshu Mutyalu

I am a PhD student at Knoxville, Department of Biosystems Engineering and Soil Science with Biogeochemical Nutrient Cycling Lab . I am supervised by Dr. Debasish Saha. I also collaborate with Dr. Neal Eash, as a Teaching Assistaint.

I obtained my Masters in Soil Science & Agricultural Chemistry from Sam Higginbottom University of Agriculture, Technology and Sciences where I was a part of the Auton Lab and the Argo AI Center for Autonomous Vehicles Research advised by Dr. Jeff Schneider. Before this I obtained by bachelors in Computer Science at Manipal Institute of Technology. I previously interned at NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory (JPL) where I worked on learning heuristics for more efficient Mars Rover motion planning. I also interned at LLNL where I worked on scaling GFlowNets training for diffusion samplers.

I am broadly interested in topics related to reinforcement learning, reasoning, and probabilistic inference. If you are interested in working with me, please reach out by email!
